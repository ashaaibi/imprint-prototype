Custom image cursor

url(…)
SVG target, hotspot 14 14

Custom cursors need a hotspot and a mandatory keyword fallback:
cursor: url('icon.svg') 4 4, pointer;
Keep them ≤ 128×128 (32×32 is safest cross-browser), and always supply the fallback or the rule is ignored.
